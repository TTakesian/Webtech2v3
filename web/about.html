ABOUT US

In November, the fictional music festival Novemberfest is organized at the Volkspark. Various national and internationally known artists and bands will play at this festival. Novemberfest is orginized each year on the second Saturday and Sunday of November by sponsors such as MOJO Concerts, large.nl and LOC7000.

Our first festival organization was in 1999, Within Temptation, Nightwish and Epica were our first bands that played on that year. Now we have many more bands that want to joing our organization

This year we proudly present our 6th festival, we have 4 bands that will play at Volkspart. The 4 bands are Within Temptation, Nightwish, Metallica and Parkway Drive! Come and join us! buy your tickets here!
