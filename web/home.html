FortaRock returns in 2017!

26-10-2017

The heaviest festival of the year in Holland returns!! In 2018 FortaRock will take place on Fridag 1 June & Saturday 2 June in Goffertpark, Nijmegen!

Nightwish confirmed for FortaRock 2018

13-09-2017

Finally, after a long wait we can announce the first name for FortaRock 2018: NIGHTWISH! The band will come to the Goffertpark in Nijmegen for a 2018 exclusive show Saturday June 2nd. This is just the beginning, there is more to follow for next years edition!

Within Temptation confirmed for Saturday June 4

06-8-2017

The Dutch symphonic metal- and rock band Within Temptation needs no introduction. It is actually surprising that the band led by the charismatic vocalist Sharon den Adel has not performed previously on FortaRock. But in 2017 it will finally happen and the band will close the Saturday.
