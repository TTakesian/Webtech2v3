Novemberfest returns in 2017!

10-08-2017

The heaviest festival of the year in Holland returns!! In 2017 Novemberfest will take place on Saturday 9 November & Sunday 10 November in Goffertpark, Nijmegen!

Nightwish confirmed for Novemberfest 2017

15-08-2017

Finally, after a long wait we can announce the first name for Novemberfest 2017: NIGHTWISH! The band will come to the Goffertpark in Nijmegen for a 2017 exclusive show Saturday 9 November. This is just the beginning, there is more to follow!

Within Temptation confirmed for Sunday 10 November

20-8-2017

The Dutch symphonic metal- and rock band Within Temptation needs no introduction. It is actually surprising that the band led by the charismatic vocalist Sharon den Adel has not performed previously on Novemberfest. But in 2017 it will finally happen.
